SAFETY

Tetranex believes that at every level, the health and safety of each employee and contractor is of the utmost importance. With the primary goal of our safety program being a safe working environment, every employee, supervisor, and manager is committed to supporting the HS&E Program. Injury prevention and maintaining a safe and healthy working environment for all employees benefits not only our resources, but also partners and ultimately our clients.

Tetranex retains COR registration for our Safety Policy by partnering with the Alberta Association for Safety Partnerships (AASP). In addition to our professional registrations, we also carry current Workers Compensation Board (WCB) coverage within British Columbia, Alberta, Saskatchewan and PEI.

In addition to our internal safety program, we are an active member of ISNetworld, ComplyWorks as well as Avetta and utilize these services to ensure that all pertinent safety aspects are up to date and that all safety statistics are appropriately tracked.

One key performance indicator of the Tetranex safety program is that we have never had a work-related lost time injury. This amounts to over 1.6 million hours safely worked. This is a stat we take very seriously and are committed to maintain.
